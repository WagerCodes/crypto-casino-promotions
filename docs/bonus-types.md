# Common Bonus Types in Crypto Casinos

Crypto casino promotions are offered in different formats. This document explains the most common bonus types and what they typically involve.

The purpose is informational, not promotional.

---

## Welcome bonuses

Welcome bonuses are usually offered to new users after registration or an initial deposit.

They may include:
- Deposit match bonuses
- Tiered rewards unlocked over multiple deposits
- Bundled promotions combining different incentives

Eligibility and structure vary by platform.

---

---

## Cashback and rakeback

Cashback or rakeback systems return a portion of losses or wagering activity back to the user.

Key points:
- Calculations are usually percentage-based
- Returns may be credited daily, weekly, or monthly
- Some systems apply caps or exclusions

These systems are often ongoing rather than one-time bonuses.

---

## Free spins and free cases

Some platforms offer free spins or free cases tied to:
- registration
- specific games or features
- limited promotional periods

Free rewards may come with usage restrictions or limits.

---

## Reward programs and tiers

Many crypto casinos use reward tiers or loyalty programs.

These programs may include:
- Level-based benefits
- Increased rakeback rates
- Access to exclusive promotions

Progression is typically based on wagering activity.

---

## Important considerations

Before participating in any bonus, users should review:
- eligibility rules
- wagering or usage requirements
- caps, limits, or exclusions
- withdrawal conditions

Understanding these terms helps avoid misunderstandings.

---

## Responsible use

Bonuses are optional and may encourage increased activity.

Users should set personal limits and review official terms before participating.
